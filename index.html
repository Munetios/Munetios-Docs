Getting Started

Welcome to Munetios Documentation! This guide will help you get started with using Munetios, the privacy-focused search engine.

To start using Munetios, simply go to www.munetios.com and enter your search query in the search bar.

Introduction of Apps

Munetios offers a variety of apps to enhance your search experience. These include:

  • Munetios Search – The main search engine.
  • Munetios OmniWrite – A document creation and editing app.
  • Munetios Photos – A photo management and editing app.
  • Munetios Calendar – A calendar app to manage your events.
  • Munetios Tasks – A task management app to keep track of your to-dos.
  • Munetios Notes – A note-taking app to capture your thoughts and ideas.
  • Munetios Calculator – A calculator app for quick calculations.
  • Munetios Notebook – A notebook app for organizing your notes.
  • Munetios Weather – A weather app to check the latest weather updates.

Search Tips

  • Use bookmarks to save your favorite searches.
  • Use Voice Mode to perform searches using your voice.
  • Use Custom Themes to personalize the appearance of Munetios.
  • Use School Mode to filter out inappropriate content.
  • Use Commands to perform specific actions quickly.

Calendar

Munetios Calendar helps you manage your events and schedules securely and privately.

How to Use Munetios Calendar:

  • To add an event, click on the + icon then fill in the event details.
  • To edit an event, click on the event icon then click edit button then make your changes.
  • To delete an event, click on the event icon then click delete button.
  • To view your calendar, click on the view button.

Tips for using Munetios Calendar effectively:

  • Turn on Desktop Notifications to stay updated on your events.
  • Create Calendars to organize different types of events.
  • Add Birthday Reminders to never miss important dates.
  • Share Events with other platforms.

FAQs about Munetios Calendar:

Is Munetios Calendar private?

Yes, Munetios Calendar is designed with privacy in mind. Your data is never sold or shared without your consent.

Can I sync Munetios Calendar with other calendar apps?

Currently, Munetios Calendar does not support syncing with other calendar apps.

How can I create calendars?

To create a calendar, click on the "Create Calendar" button in the calendar app and follow the prompts.

Do I need an account to use Munetios Calendar?

No, you do not need an account to use Munetios Calendar. It is free and open to all users.

Is there a mobile app?

As of now, Munetios Calendar does not have a dedicated mobile app.

OmniWrite

Munetios OmniWrite is a powerful document creation and editing app that prioritizes your privacy.

How to Use Munetios OmniWrite:

  • To create a document, click on the plus icon in the sidebar at top right corner. then it will create a document for you to start editing.
  • To edit a document, simply click on the document you want to edit from the sidebar.
  • To save a document, currently it has autosave functionality.
  • To export/import documents, click on the settings button then click export or import.
  • To format text, use the toolbar at the top of the document editor.

Tips for using Munetios OmniWrite effectively:

  • Use the images feature and upload images to quick insert image in your documents.
  • Use headings and formatting options to organize your content.
  • Use Custom Color themes to personalize your experience.

FAQs about Munetios OmniWrite:

Is Munetios OmniWrite private?

Yes, Munetios OmniWrite is designed with privacy in mind and encrypted. Your documents are never sold or shared without your consent.

Can I collaborate with others on documents?

Currently, Munetios OmniWrite does not support real-time collaboration on documents.

How can I export my documents?

To export a document, click on the settings button in the sidebar, then click export button.

Do I need an account to use Munetios OmniWrite?

No, you do not need an account to use Munetios OmniWrite. It is free and open to all users.

Is there a mobile app?

As of now, Munetios OmniWrite does not have a dedicated mobile app.

Weather

Munetios Weather gives you the latest weather updates without tracking your location.

How to Use Munetios Weather:

  • To check the weather, enter your location in the search bar.
  • To view detailed forecasts, scroll down on the weather page.
  • To switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit, click on settings button then select your preferred unit.

Tips for using Munetios Weather effectively:

  • Save your favorite locations for quick access.
  • Use daylight savings (Inaccurate during transitions).

FAQs about Munetios Weather:

Is Munetios Weather private?

Yes, Munetios Weather is designed with privacy in mind. Your location data is never sold or shared without your consent.

Which API that Munetios Weather uses?

Munetios Weather uses the Open-Meteo API to provide accurate weather data.

Do I need an account to use Munetios Weather?

No, you do not need an account to use Munetios Weather. It is free and open to all users.
